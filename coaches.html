SHANE WELLS





Shane is a lifelong fitness enthusiast with an extensive history in organized sports, weight training, competitive endurance events and obstacle course running. His impassioned CrossFit Journey began in 2015 with CrossFit Fox Den. He gained his LvL 1 in 2017 and has been empowering others through his belief that at any level the path to success starts with a solid fundamental base with proper technique and movement competence no matter the modality goal. Shane is driven by the success of anyone seeking self betterment and takes great pride in ensuring everyone advances in their individualized fitness journey.

ANDREA JACKSON





Andrea Jackson is a CrossFit Level 1 trainer and a licensed Physical Therapist Assistant who has been practicing physical therapy since 2007. She has traveled all over the country working in skilled nursing facilities and has experience in rehabilitating spinal cord injuries, strokes, total joint replacements, and many other orthopedic and neurological disorders. Andrea is passionate about health and fitness, and teaching others to live a healthy lifestyle to ensure a better quality of life now and in the future.
